Hostel Overview — Cauvery Bhawan

  1. Cauvery Bhawan has a capacity of 285 students. A total of 292 rooms are available for occupancy; all rooms are single-seater. The hostel is a three-storey building with three blocks: A, B, and C.
  2. The hostel has one large dining hall, 1 common room, 6 guest rooms, 1 warden office, and 1 visitors’ room, surrounded by green lawns.
Blocks A, B, C 292 single rooms Dining hall 6 guest rooms

Hostel Staff Overview

  1. For day-to-day management, a Supervisor, an Assistant Hostel Supervisor, and day/night attendants are available in the hostel 24×7 as per the duty roster.
  2. Security guards are deployed 24 hours at the girls’ hostel barrier.
  3. Mess staff and MTW staff serve meals at regular mess timings. In addition, two sweepers are placed on each floor for corridors and washrooms, and one gardener maintains the hostel lawns.
